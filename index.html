Ashish Pathak


I am a recent graduate from State University of New York at Plattsburgh where I worked on projects ranging from vertical search engine to mobile application development. In 2015, I joined the UVA School of Medicine as a Web Application Developer.

My background in Computer Science includes undergraduate research optimizing search ranking based on the conversation-structure of an online discussion.

Outside work, my interests include programming for mobile and wearable devices and supporting Liverpool FC. Find out more about me after the break.
